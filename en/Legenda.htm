  The Legend of champions
 
 

The Legend of champions is a set of stories about small software firm where term championism was born. Authors of this site spent part of their life working for that firm and there they had unique opportunity to meet  people whose professional skills and personality perfectly match the definition of real world-class champions. When they got out of that hell, they felt that keeping that experience inside would be a terrible burden on them and that somehow they shoud tell the whole world about the championism and champions. The result of their thoughts is this site and this Legend you are about to read.

The Legend is not complete because we still have stories in our memory that have yet to be written. We are working on completing the Legend, but we hope that what we wrote so far would be enough to give you an idea how it was to work for genuine champ firm and with true world-class champions. You will be notified through news page when new stories arrive.

Names in the Legend are not real, but everything else is. Have a nice reading!
